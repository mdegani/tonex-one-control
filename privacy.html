Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

Tonex One Control (tonex.markbuilds.ca) is a free, open-source tool for editing parameters on the IK Multimedia TONEX ONE guitar pedal. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data stored in your browser

By default, all data stays on your device. The app uses your browser's localStorage to persist:

  • Pedal preset settings and parameter values
  • Snapshots (saved parameter configurations per tone model)
  • Rig descriptions (guitars, pedals, pedalboard text you enter)
  • Imported preset metadata (model names, amp/cab descriptions)

This data is never transmitted anywhere unless you choose to sign in (see below). You can clear it at any time through your browser settings or the "Clear All Snapshots" button in the app.

Google sign-in and cloud sync

Signing in is optional. If you sign in with Google, the app uses Firebase Authentication (operated by Google) to verify your identity. The following information is received from Google and used by the app:

  • Your email address (displayed in the app so you know which account is signed in)
  • Your profile photo URL (displayed as an avatar in the header)
  • A unique user ID (used to associate your cloud data with your account)

When signed in, your snapshots are synced to Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud) so you can access them across devices. Firestore data is stored in Google's data centers and governed by Firebase Terms of Service and Google's Privacy Policy.

Signing out stops cloud sync. Your snapshots remain available locally in localStorage.

Phone remote control

The QR code sharing feature uses Firebase Realtime Database to relay pedal state between your computer and phone. This data includes:

  • Current preset parameters and values
  • Slider commands sent from the phone

Session data is ephemeral — it is automatically deleted when either device disconnects. No account is required to use this feature.

USB communication

The app communicates directly with your TONEX ONE pedal over USB using the WebSerial API. All communication happens locally between your browser and the pedal. No pedal data is sent to any server.

Analytics

Cloudflare Web Analytics is used to measure page visits. It does not use cookies, does not track individual users, and does not collect personal information. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

  • localStorage — Persists until you clear your browser data or use the in-app clear button.
  • Cloud snapshots — Stored in Firestore while your account exists. You can delete individual snapshots or all snapshots from the app.
  • Relay sessions — Deleted automatically on disconnect.

To delete all cloud data, sign in and use "Clear All Snapshots" in Settings, then sign out.

No selling or sharing

Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Data is only transmitted to the third-party services listed above for their stated purposes.
